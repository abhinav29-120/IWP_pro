Keeping Yourself Safe

Get an anti-phishing antivirus
Antiviruses, specially those which provide a certain level of protection against phishing, can be of great aid to the users in preventing them falling prey to such attacks.
Be aware of fraudulent emials and messages
One of the most important aspects of keeping yourself safe from such attacks is being alert when accessing your e-mail or going through your messages and avoiding clicking on links until you are absolutely sure about them.
Identifying legitimate offers from the faudulent ones
Sending unbelivable offers as a bait to users is one of the most used techniques by attackers and users fall prey to such attacks the most. Being alert while going through such baits is of utmost importance in this fight against phishing.
Confirm authenticity of a website
If you have any doubts regarding a url that you might have received, check it through our site or from any site for that matter to make sure the url is authentic and the website is safe to vist.
