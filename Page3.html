Types of Phishing

Spear Phishing
These are phishing attempts directed at a particular individual or organization. Generally involves attackers gathering personal information about the targets before planning the attack.
Clone Phishing
This type involves cloning a previously delivered, legitimate e-mail havinga link and replacing the link with a malicious one and then sending it in such a way that it appears to be sent from the original sender, thus making the user to believe the ink to be legitimate.
SMS Phishing
SMS Phishing or Smishing is a technique where users are sent certain texts which inform them about a lottery or in some cases, a threat to their account security and then making them reveal confidential information such as account credentials or credit card numbers, their bank account numbers etc.
Voice Phishing
Voice Phishig or Vishing involves attackers pretending to be employees of a bank or other organizations and then asking users to tell about their pin numbers or other confidential details so as to have their accounts functioning without any hassle.
